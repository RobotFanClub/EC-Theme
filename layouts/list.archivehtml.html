Everyone's Creative began its life as Robot Fan Club, a blog/newsletter originally about a video game project, then evolving into being about my artwork and thoughts more generally.As Robot Fan Club evolved, I realized I was less interested in sharing updates on my work and more interested in exploring the broader intersections of art, creativity, politics and our place in it all.
That's why I renamed the project Everyone's Creative, but the core of a lot of the spirit of this work can still be found in these older posts. For that reason, and to help reveal the reality of how this project has evolved, the Robot Fan Club archive is still found below.
